What size dumpster do I need in Dillon, SC?
For small cleanouts, a 10-yard handles 1-2 rooms. Kitchen or bathroom remodels need a 15-20 yard. Full home renovations and roofing projects use 30-40 yards. Select Not Sure in the form and a local provider will recommend the right size.

Do I need a permit for a dumpster in Dillon, SC?
