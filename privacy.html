Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Florence SC Services ("we," "us," or "our") operates florencescservices.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

We collect information you voluntarily provide when submitting a quote request, including name, phone number, email address, service address or ZIP, project details, and consent acknowledgment. We may also collect limited technical data (browser type, IP address, pages visited) for basic analytics.

We use your information to connect you with local roll-off dumpster rental providers, respond to your inquiries, and improve our website.

We share your contact and project information with local dumpster rental providers solely to fulfill your quote request. We do not sell your personal information to third-party marketers or data brokers.

We retain your information for a reasonable period to fulfill your request. You may request deletion by contacting us at the email below.

6. Cookies

We may use basic cookies for functionality and analytics. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure.
