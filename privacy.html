Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Florence SC Services LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates florencescservices.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when submitting a quote request or opting in to our text messaging service, including name, phone number, email address, service address or ZIP, project details, and consent acknowledgment. We may also collect limited technical data (browser type, IP address, pages visited) for basic analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to connect you with local roll-off dumpster rental providers, respond to your inquiries, communicate with you via phone calls and text messages about our services, and improve our website.

3. Text Message Service

Florence SC Services LLC offers a text messaging service to communicate with prospective and current clients regarding lead generation services, follow-ups on inquiries, and account updates.

Consent

By opting in to our text messaging service — whether by submitting a form on our website with the consent checkbox selected, providing verbal consent during a phone call and confirming via text reply, or texting us directly — you agree to receive text messages from Florence SC Services LLC at the phone number you provided. Consent is not required as a condition of purchasing any goods or services. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are exempt from any sharing described in this policy.

Message Frequency & Rates

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation message. You may also contact us by email or phone to request removal.

Mobile Information Sharing

We respect your privacy. We use the information you provide to send and respond to your mobile messages. This includes sharing it with platform providers, phone companies, and other vendors who assist us in delivering messages. Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We may disclose information to satisfy legal, regulatory, or governmental requests, avoid liability, or protect our rights or property.

Relationship to General Privacy Policy

This section applies specifically to your use of our text messaging service and does not modify the remaining sections of this Privacy Policy, which may govern our relationship with you in other contexts.

4. Sharing Your Information

We share your contact and project information with local dumpster rental providers solely to fulfill your quote request. We do not sell your personal information to third-party marketers or data brokers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for a reasonable period to fulfill your request. Consent records related to text messaging are retained for a minimum of five years for compliance purposes. You may request deletion of other personal data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for form processing, text messaging, call tracking, and email delivery. These services have their own privacy policies.

7. Cookies

We may use basic cookies for functionality and analytics. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

11. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
