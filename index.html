Quick Answers

For a small cleanout or bathroom remodel, a 10-yard usually works. Kitchen remodels and medium projects often need 20 yards. Whole-home renovations or large debris loads typically need 30–40 yards. Not sure? Select "Help Me Choose" in the form and a provider will guide you. See our size chart →
Pricing depends on size, debris type, rental duration, and the provider. Small dumpsters typically start around $250–$450 for a week. Final pricing is set by the local provider. See our pricing guide →
