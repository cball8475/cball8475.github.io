Common Questions

For small cleanouts, a 20-yard handles most single-room cleanouts, garage projects, and small remodels. Kitchen and bathroom renovations usually need 20–30 yards. Full home remodels and roofing jobs typically need 30 yards. Large construction or demolition: 40 yards. Select "Not Sure" in the form and a local provider will recommend the right size.
Prices in the Florence area typically range from $400–$450 for a 20-yard up to $650–$700 for a 40-yard, plus a fuel surcharge. Final pricing depends on size, debris type, rental duration, and provider. Submit a request for accurate local pricing.
Most local Florence County providers can deliver within 24–48 hours. Same-day delivery is sometimes available for urgent projects. Select "ASAP" in the timeline field and we'll prioritize connecting you with a provider who can meet your schedule.
