Right-Sizing Your Dumpster for Residential Projects

Ordering too large wastes money. Ordering too small means a second delivery fee. Here's how to get it right the first time for common Darlington and Hartsville home projects:

Recommended Sizes by Project Type

  • 10-yard dumpster — Single-room cleanout, garage cleanout, small bathroom remodel (approx. 3 pickup truck loads)
  • 20-yard dumpster — Full home cleanout, kitchen renovation, deck removal (approx. 6 pickup truck loads)
  • 30-yard dumpster — Estate cleanouts, large home remodels, major landscaping (approx. 9 pickup truck loads)
💡 Not sure? When in doubt, size up. The cost difference between a 10 and 20-yard container is usually $50–$100 — far less than paying for a second delivery.

Protecting Your Driveway During Delivery

One of the most overlooked aspects of residential dumpster rental is driveway protection. A loaded roll-off container can weigh several tons and cause damage to concrete or asphalt surfaces, especially older driveways common in Darlington and Hartsville neighborhoods.

Driveway Protection Tips

  • Ask your provider if they use boards or plywood under the container feet
  • Avoid placement on cracked or aging concrete — weight can worsen existing damage
  • Consider placing on gravel or grass if your driveway is older
  • Never place on septic tank lids or utility covers
  • Take photos of your driveway before delivery as documentation

Local Delivery: What to Expect in Darlington & Hartsville

Scheduling and Timing

  • Most local providers offer same-day or next-day delivery in Darlington and Hartsville
  • Delivery windows are typically morning (7am–12pm) or afternoon (12pm–5pm)
  • Clear the placement area of vehicles and obstacles before the driver arrives

Placement Requirements

  • The container needs approximately 10 feet of width and 22 feet of length
  • Overhead clearance of at least 23 feet is required for the truck
  • Low-hanging trees, power lines, and carports can restrict placement options

What Homeowners Can and Cannot Dispose Of

✅ Accepted Items

  • Furniture and mattresses
  • Appliances (confirm refrigerants)
  • Clothing and household goods
  • Yard waste and landscaping debris
  • Flooring, carpet, and tile
  • General junk and clutter
  • Wood and lumber scraps

❌ Not Accepted

  • Paint and paint cans
  • Propane tanks
  • Car batteries and tires
  • Hazardous chemicals
  • Electronics (e-waste)
  • Asbestos materials
  • Medical waste
