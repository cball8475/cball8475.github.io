Partner Outreach Policy

How we find and contact local dumpster rental companies — and what to expect when we reach out.

Who this page is for: Dumpster rental operators in Florence, South Carolina and the surrounding Pee Dee region who have received — or may receive — outreach from us about a lead generation partnership.

What We Do

Florence SC Services (florencescservices.com) is a local lead generation business. We connect residents and contractors in the Pee Dee region who need dumpster rentals with independent local operators who can serve them.

We are not a dumpster rental company ourselves. We capture customer leads — people who are actively looking to rent a dumpster — and refer those leads to partner operators in our network.

How We Find Businesses to Contact

We identify dumpster rental companies through publicly available sources, including:

  • Google Maps and Google Business Profile — businesses listed as dumpster rental or waste removal services in Florence, Darlington, Hartsville, Marion, Lake City, Dillon, and surrounding Pee Dee communities.

  • Yelp and other online directories — publicly listed local operators in our service area.

  • General web search — websites and local business listings found through standard internet research.

We do not purchase contact lists or use data brokers. Every business we contact has a publicly available presence online.

How We Reach Out

Initial contact is made by phone call or text message to the business phone number listed in the company's public profile. Our outreach is B2B only — we contact business owners and operators, not consumers.

Our messages introduce who we are, explain what we do, and invite the business to connect if they're interested in receiving referral leads. There is no obligation or cost to receive our outreach.

Opt-Out Instructions

Every text message we send includes clear opt-out instructions. Businesses that do not wish to receive further messages can reply STOP at any time. We will immediately honor all opt-out requests and will not send additional messages to that number.

You may also contact us directly at charlie@florencescservices.com to be removed from outreach.

Scope of Outreach

Our outreach is limited to:

  • Business-to-business (B2B) only. We contact dumpster rental companies and waste removal operators — not consumers or residential customers.

  • Pee Dee region, South Carolina. Our geographic focus is Florence, Darlington, Hartsville, Lake City, Marion, Dillon, and nearby communities.

  • Lead generation partnership only. We are not soliciting sales, collecting payments, or promoting unrelated products or services.

Questions About Our Outreach?
