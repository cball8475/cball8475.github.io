Do You Need a Permit for a Dumpster in Florence SC?

Quick guide to Florence city and county dumpster placement rules.

Home › Blog › Dumpster Permit Guide

Updated March 5, 2026 · By Florence SC Services

One of the most common questions we get from Florence County residents: "Do I need a permit for a dumpster?" The short answer depends entirely on where you place it.

The Simple Rule

✅ Private property (driveway, yard) = No permit needed

⚠️ Public street or right-of-way = Permit required

When You DON'T Need a Permit

If you can place the dumpster entirely on your own property — your driveway, parking area, or yard — you do not need a permit in the City of Florence or unincorporated Florence County. This covers the vast majority of residential dumpster rentals.

When You DO Need a Permit

You'll need to contact city or county offices if:

  • The dumpster must be placed on a public street
  • It extends into the public right-of-way (sidewalk, curb area)
  • You're in a commercial zone with specific placement rules
  • Your neighborhood has HOA restrictions (not a government permit, but still required)

How to Get a Dumpster Permit in Florence

  1. Contact the City of Florence Public Works department
  2. Provide the placement address and dumpster size
  3. Pay any applicable permit fees
  4. Place the dumpster according to any conditions (reflective cones, time limits, etc.)

Processing usually takes 1-2 business days. Plan ahead if you need street placement.

HOA Considerations

Several Florence SC neighborhoods and subdivisions have HOA rules about dumpster placement. Common restrictions include:

  • Maximum rental duration (often 7-14 days)
  • Must be placed in driveway, not visible from street (not always possible)
  • Prior written notification to the HOA board
  • Specific size limitations

Tip: Check your HOA covenants before ordering and send a quick email to your HOA board. Most are fine with dumpsters for active renovation projects.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a permit for a dumpster on my driveway in Florence SC?
No. Dumpsters placed on private property such as your driveway or yard do not require a permit in the City of Florence or Florence County.
How much does a dumpster permit cost in Florence SC?
Contact the City of Florence Public Works department for current permit fees. Fees are typically nominal and the process takes 1-2 business days.
Can my HOA prevent me from getting a dumpster?
Many Florence SC HOAs have rules about dumpster placement and duration. Check your HOA covenants before ordering and notify your HOA board. Most allow dumpsters for active renovation projects.
