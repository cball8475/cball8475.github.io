Spring Cleaning in Florence SC: Your Complete Dumpster Rental Guide

Everything Pee Dee homeowners need to know about renting a dumpster for spring cleanout season.

Updated March 8, 2026 · By Florence SC Services

Spring is the busiest season for dumpster rentals in Florence County. Warmer weather means garage cleanouts, yard projects, and home renovations kick into high gear across the Pee Dee region. Here's how to plan your spring cleanout efficiently and save money.

Why Spring is Peak Dumpster Season in Florence

Florence SC's mild March-May weather (highs in the 70s-80s) makes it perfect for outdoor projects. Local providers report 40-60% higher demand from March through June compared to winter months. That means two things for you:

  • Book 3-5 days ahead — same-day delivery gets harder as spring progresses
  • Weekdays are easier — Saturday delivery slots fill up first

Best Dumpster Size for Spring Cleanouts

Most Florence homeowners doing a spring cleanout need a 10 or 15-yard dumpster. Here's a quick sizing guide:

What You Can Toss in a Spring Cleanout Dumpster

Most household items are fair game for a roll-off dumpster:

  • Old furniture (sofas, beds, dressers, tables)
  • Appliances (confirm Freon removal for fridges/ACs)
  • Yard debris, branches, and landscaping waste
  • Old carpeting and flooring
  • Clothes, toys, and general household junk
  • Shed and garage contents

Not accepted: paint, chemicals, tires, electronics, batteries, and hazardous materials. Florence County has a household hazardous waste program for those items.

5 Money-Saving Tips for Spring Dumpster Rental

  1. Combine with neighbors — split a 20-yard dumpster with your next-door neighbor and cut costs in half.
  2. Donate first — Habitat for Humanity ReStore in Florence accepts furniture and building materials. Less in the dumpster = lower weight fees.
  3. Get your quote early — March pricing is typically 5-10% lower than May/June when demand peaks.
  4. Pack smart — break down boxes, disassemble furniture, and use the swing door to walk heavy items in first.
  5. Know your weight limit — overage fees ($40-$75/ton) add up fast. Keep heavy items like dirt and concrete out unless you've specifically ordered a heavy-debris dumpster.
