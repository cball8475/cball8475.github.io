Do You Need a Permit for a Dumpster in Florence SC?

Whether you need a permit depends on where you place the container:

  • Private property (driveway, yard) — No permit required in Florence SC
  • Public street or right-of-way — Permit required from City of Florence Public Works
  • Florence County roads — Contact Florence County for right-of-way requirements
💡 Easy rule: If you can fit it on your driveway, you don't need a permit. If it has to go on the street, call Public Works first.

Delivery & Placement Tips for Florence SC

Placement Requirements

  • Clear a space at least 10 feet wide and 22 feet long
  • Ensure 23 feet of overhead clearance for the delivery truck
  • Keep the area free of vehicles, low branches, and power lines
  • Never place over septic tank lids or utility access covers

Protecting Your Driveway

  • Ask your provider if they use boards or plywood under the container feet
  • Avoid placing on cracked or aging concrete — the weight can worsen damage
  • Take photos of your driveway before delivery as documentation

What You Can & Cannot Throw in a Florence SC Dumpster

✅ Generally Accepted

  • Household junk, furniture, and appliances (confirm refrigerants)
  • Construction debris — wood, drywall, flooring, roofing materials
  • Yard waste and landscaping debris
  • Concrete, brick, and block (confirm weight limits)
  • Carpet, tile, and general renovation waste

❌ Not Accepted

  • Liquid paint, solvents, and hazardous chemicals
  • Tires, car batteries, and motor oil
  • Propane tanks and compressed gas cylinders
  • Electronics and fluorescent bulbs
  • Asbestos-containing materials
  • Medical and biohazardous waste

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does dumpster rental cost in Florence SC?

Dumpster rental in Florence SC typically costs $250–$550 depending on size. A 10-yard averages $250–$350, a 20-yard runs $300–$450, and a 30–40 yard container costs $400–$650. Prices vary by provider, debris type, and rental duration.

What size dumpster do I need?

Use a 10-yard for small cleanouts (about 3 pickup truck loads), a 20-yard for kitchen remodels and deck removals (6 loads), a 30-yard for full home renovations and roofing projects (9 loads), and a 40-yard for construction and demolition (12+ loads).

Do I need a permit for a dumpster in Florence SC?

If the dumpster is on your private property (driveway or yard), no permit is required. If it goes on a public street, you'll need a permit from the City of Florence Public Works department.

How fast can I get a dumpster delivered in Florence SC?

Most local Florence SC providers offer same-day or next-day delivery. Submit a quote request and a provider will contact you within 2 hours. For urgent requests, call (843) 938-0480.

Do you serve Darlington, Hartsville, and other Pee Dee cities?

Yes. We connect customers with local providers throughout the Pee Dee region including Florence, Darlington, Hartsville, Lake City, Marion, Dillon, Mullins, and surrounding communities in South Carolina.

What's the difference between a 20-yard and 30-yard dumpster?

A 20-yard holds about 6 pickup truck loads and is ideal for single-room renovations and medium cleanouts. A 30-yard holds about 9 loads and is better for full home remodels, large estate cleanouts, and roofing projects. When in doubt, size up.
