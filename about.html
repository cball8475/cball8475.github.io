About Florence SC Services

A free, local quote-request service — we help you find the right dumpster, not sell you one.

What We Do

How We Help

Florence SC Services is a free quote-request and matching service for residents and businesses in Florence, South Carolina and the surrounding Pee Dee region. We are not a dumpster rental company — we help you find one.

When you submit a request, we review your project details and connect you with local roll-off providers who serve your address. You work directly with the provider for pricing, scheduling, delivery, and pickup.

Our goal: make it faster and less frustrating to get a reliable roll-off dumpster — without the runaround of calling five companies yourself.

Disclaimer:

Florence SC Services is a lead generation and quote-request service only. We do not own, operate, or dispatch dumpsters. Availability, scheduling, final pricing, and service terms are determined solely by the local provider. We make no guarantees regarding provider availability, response times, or pricing.

Our Commitments

How We Operate

Free & No Obligation

Submitting a request costs nothing. You're never obligated to book with any provider we connect you with.

No Hidden Fees

We don't charge consumers. We may receive a referral fee from providers — this never affects the quote you receive.

Local Focus

We focus on Florence County and the Pee Dee region — not a national marketplace with providers who don't know the area.

Honest Information

No invented prices, fake reviews, or made-up certifications. Just accurate guidance to help you make a good decision.
