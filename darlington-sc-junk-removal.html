What We Take

What We Haul in Darlington, SC

If it is not hazardous, the crew can usually haul it. Common jobs in the Darlington area:

  • Furniture — couches, mattresses, dressers, tables
  • Appliances — refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves
  • Full house, garage, and basement cleanouts
  • Estate and move-out cleanouts
  • Yard waste, brush, and storm debris
  • Construction and renovation debris
  • Hot tubs, sheds, decks, and playsets
  • General clutter and single-item pickups

What we can’t take

  • Hazardous materials (paint, solvents, chemicals, pesticides)
  • Free liquids of any kind
  • Medical or biohazard waste
  • Asbestos-containing materials

Which Do You Need

Junk Removal or a Dumpster?

If you have a pile of stuff you want gone today without lifting it yourself, junk removal is the move — the crew loads it and hauls it. If you have a multi-day project and want to fill a container on your own schedule, a roll-off dumpster is usually cheaper per load.

Rule of thumb: a few items or one room → junk removal. A whole renovation or roofing job → rent a dumpster in Darlington.
